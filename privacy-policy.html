Bitwise — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Bitwise ("the App") is a binary logic puzzle game developed by Aytunc Sevren ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App does not require account creation, login, or any form of registration.

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Game statistics (scores, completion times, win/loss records)
  • Puzzle progress and game state
  • App preferences and settings (theme, language, sound)
  • Achievement progress
  • Hint balance

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Apple Game Center

If you choose to use Game Center features, Apple may collect your profile information and gameplay data according to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases)

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

3. Data Storage & iCloud Sync

Game data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate any servers or databases.

If you enable iCloud sync, your game data will be synced across your devices via Apple's CloudKit service. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is handled according to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

You may also export your data as a JSON file for personal backup. This export stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be permanently removed. iCloud data may persist until you manually remove it from your iCloud account.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect any personal data, the App complies with COPPA and similar regulations.

5. Push Notifications

The App may request permission to send local push notifications to remind you about daily puzzles. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and no data is sent to any server. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

6. Your Rights

  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the App
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • You can disable Game Center in your device settings
  • You can disable notifications in your device settings
  • You can disable iCloud sync in the App settings

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at:

Email: aytuncsevren@gmail.com
